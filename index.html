How I Work

My design process

Research

Understand the end goals and direction for the project, including target style, colors, and platform.

Sketch

Brainstorm ideas by sketching them out to get a sense of visual direction and project scope.

Refine

Move designs into Illustrator or Photoshop and begin adding details and creating mock-ups.

Polish

Get feedback and polish designs by reiterating details and getting assets ready implementation.

About Toni

Creative designer and game enthusiast

Bio

After graduating early from the University of California in San Diego in 2011, I utilized my B.A. in media arts and computing to enroll in the Academy of Art University to study game design. Currently, I'm enrolled as a part-time graduate student and am working to complete my thesis projects: a series of education and cognitively stimulating mobile games design.

I'm a versatile designer with a penchant for tackling new challenges and mediums. Aside from my professional work experiences, I involve myself in various side projects and collaborations ranging from mobile game projects to website development. When I find a good cause, I like to lend my services as a voluntary visual designer, the most recent being with KitTea in San Francisco.

To see more about my mobile game thesis projects, keep scrolling! My goal for these projects is to develop useful and meaninful products that are educational and fun. All of them are expected to ship within the year.
