How I Work

My creative process

Sketch

Understand the end goal from the start and begin sketching out ideas. By getting concepts down on paper, I'm able to brainstorm ideas about the project. This step also enables me to flesh out the project scope and start developing project aesthetics.

Wireframe & Prototype

Begin moving concepts into their appropriate mediums. For example, visual concepts will move to Illustrator, while mobile projects will move to XCode. This is where I start refining my designs and start creating mock-ups, wireframes, and bare-bone products.

Iteration

Adapt designs to feedback and product goals. Should the goal change from the start of the project, I will reiterate my design to conform to new goals. Feedback is vital to every step of the creative process, as is being a flexible designer and developer.

Final Polish

Prepare final project assets. Depending on the type of project, this could mean creating a presentation deck, shipping a mobile application, or optimizing image files for web. In every case, it's important to ensure that the end product is ready.
